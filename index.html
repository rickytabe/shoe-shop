Our Story

Meet The Visionary

Founded in 2024 by Mr Charles Tabe, StepUp began as a small boutique on Molyko Street, Buea, with a passion for bringing world-class footwear to Cameroon.

Our Mission

To elevate Cameroonian fashion by combining global trends with local craftsmanship, providing affordable luxury that stands the test of time.

Our Vision

To become the leading footwear destination in Central Africa while nurturing young Cameroonian designers.

Proudly based in Buea, Cameroon, we source local materials where possible and support regional artisans through our manufacturing partnerships.
